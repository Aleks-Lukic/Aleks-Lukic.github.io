The dogs need our support!


Sava Shelter as a non-profit organization is meant to be purely funded by donations. Nevertheless, me and my family already invested a huge amount of time and money into the project. We just want to help the dogs as quickly as possible!

If you would like to support us, you can do so in different ways:

1. Contact us! We would love to get to know you and tell you more about Sava Shelter.
2. Spread the word! The more people know about Sava Shelter, the better the future for stray dogs.
3. Donate a small amount to one of the following organization accounts. Even the smallest donation helps us!
